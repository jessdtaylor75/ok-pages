AERPASS Stores your most sensitive information in a Vault that can never be accessed without identity verification.
  • Verification is based on a scan of a government issued ID card, driver's license, or passport.
  • AerPass wall also perform a facial scan to verify that it's really you and no one else.
  • Once you've been verified, you will be able to send and receive money, and access all services which require your true identity.
